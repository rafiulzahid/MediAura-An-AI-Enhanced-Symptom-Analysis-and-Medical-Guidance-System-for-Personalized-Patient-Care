About Us 💀

Welcome to MediAura

MediAura is an AI-powered medical assistant care system that enables seamless interaction between patients and doctors. Our system empowers users to check symptoms, monitor vital signs using IoT devices, and maintain medical records with ease.

We aim to reduce the gap between people and quality healthcare by using technology to provide fast, intelligent, and accessible solutions — anytime, anywhere.
